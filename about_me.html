ABOUT ME

Hey! My name is Adi Roitburg. I'm a computer science major AND game design / animation minor at Loyola Marymount University.

I was born in Israel, then moved to Seattle a couple years later.

I grew up with a love for video games. Some of my favorite games from my childhood included Super Mario, Plants Vs Zombies, Pokemon Black, and Destiny. My interest in video games further developed as I grew up, now becoming interested in creating games.

While I am still interested in making video games, I also became interested in other fields in computer science, such as UX Design and Machine Learning.

Other than computer science stuff, I enjoy playing tennis, bouldering, music, and video editing!
